## Who We Are

Evokita is a Malaysian pickleball gear and community brand built for players of all levels — from first-timers picking up a paddle to seasoned competitors chasing their next tournament.

We believe pickleball is better when you play it with your people. That's why we're building more than just a gear shop — we're building a community.

**Kita Rally Together.**

---

## What We Do

- **Gear** — Premium paddles, balls, bags and accessories curated for Malaysian players
- **Coaching** — Beginner clinics, drill programmes and one-on-one sessions
- **Community** — Social play sessions, mixers and tournaments across Klang Valley
