At **Evokita**, we bring the best pickleball gear and training resources to the Malaysian community — so you can play better, rally harder, and grow together.

## Paddles & Equipment
- Carbon fibre and fibreglass paddles for all skill levels
- Outdoor and indoor certified pickleballs
- Paddle bags, court shoes, and protective gear

## Coaching & Clinics
- Group coaching sessions for beginners to intermediate players
- Private one-on-one sessions with certified coaches
- Weekend intensives and holiday camp programmes

## Social Games & Community
- Organised social play sessions across Klang Valley
- Round-robin and fun tournaments open to all levels
- A growing community of players — beginners always welcome
